How Route Pilot works

Tell us your goal and constraints in plain language. Route Pilot explores tradeoffs and presents a few winning itineraries with reasoning.

1) You talk, not type

Say what you want: budget ceiling, flexible dates, baggage rules, preferred airlines, or “no red-eyes.”

  • Budget first: “Under $500” beats “March 12–15 only.”
  • Constraints: visa, max stops, minimum layover, wheelchair needs.

2) We search the weird routes

Route Pilot hunts for value the way humans do: splits, positionings, and overlooked airports.

  • Multi-city hacks: break trips into legs to unlock cheaper fare buckets.
  • Positioning flights: fly to a different hub to catch a cheaper long-haul.

3) You get options with reasoning

Not just a price — Route Pilot explains time vs money, risks, and “why this is cheaper.”

  • Overnight tradeoffs: pay $70 for a hotel to save $280 on airfare.
  • Near-airport swaps: “SFO vs OAK” or “LHR vs LGW” when it matters.
